Killed By Dhananjaya

Mindpark

November 2022 - May 2023

Mindpark began as a standalone podcast and evolved into a podcast network, featuring two distinct shows. With a combined listenership of over 2000, it gained traction from November 2022 to May 2023. Unfortunately, the project came to an end due to personal reasons and conflicts within the team.

Ehakade

Late 2021

Ehakade, launched in late 2021, aimed to provide an eCommerce solution specifically tailored for small and local businesses. The project was developed in response to the growing COVID-19 situation in Sri Lanka, with the intention of supporting businesses in adapting to the changing market conditions. However, the project faced challenges and ultimately failed due to a lack of market identification.

Bihidora

July 2019 - July 2021

Bihidora, an engaging blog/news site focused on technology, science, art, and entertainment, was active from July 2019 to July 2021. With over 1000 followers, the platform gained a dedicated readership. Unfortunately, the project had to be discontinued due to time constraints.

Ebuy Lanka International

Early 2019 - Mid 2019

Ebuy Lanka International marked Dhananjaya's first venture into the world of business. Launched in February 2019 as an e-commerce platform, the project unfortunately faced challenges and had to be closed down by mid-2019. Despite initial aspirations, Ebuy Lanka International struggled to generate sales and failed to gain traction in the competitive online marketplace. This early experience taught valuable lessons about the importance of market research, marketing strategies, and building a strong customer base for sustainable success in the e-commerce industry.
